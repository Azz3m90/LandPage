FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FastCaisse Retail POS

What makes FastCaisse the #1 certified POS in Belgium?

FastCaisse is officially registered and certified by the Belgian Federal Public Service Finance (FPS Finance). Our system meets all legal requirements for cash registers in Belgium, including secure data storage and tamper-proof transaction logging. This certification ensures complete compliance with Belgian fiscal regulations, protecting your business from legal issues.

How does barcode scanning work with FastCaisse Retail POS?

FastCaisse Retail POS seamlessly integrates with standard barcode scanners. Simply connect your scanner via USB or Bluetooth, and you can instantly scan product barcodes during checkout. The system automatically retrieves product information, price, and inventory details, making transactions fast and accurate while reducing manual data entry errors.

Can I manage multiple stores with FastCaisse Retail POS?

Yes, our Enterprise and Ultimate plans support multi-store management. You can centrally manage inventory across all locations, track sales performance by store, consolidate reporting, and synchronize data in real-time. This is perfect for retail chains or businesses with multiple outlets.

Is customer data secure with FastCaisse Retail POS?

Absolutely. We prioritize data security and are fully compliant with GDPR regulations. All customer data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We perform regular security audits, maintain secure data centers within the EU, and offer features like two-factor authentication to protect your business and customer information.

What hardware is compatible with FastCaisse Retail POS?

FastCaisse Retail POS works with a wide range of standard retail hardware including barcode scanners, receipt printers, cash drawers, customer displays, scales, and payment terminals. We provide detailed compatibility lists and can help you select the right hardware for your specific needs.
